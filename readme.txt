**Store Hours Manager For Woocommerce** using to which time store will be open and close. 

<h3>Example Woocommerce Opening Hours</h3>

    Days        		Opening Time
    Monday       		10 AM - 9 PM
    Tuesday-Saturday	9 AM - 9 PM
    Sunday				Holiday

The **Woocommerce Opening Hours & Chosen Times** Plugin enables/disables the WooCommerce checkout functionality so orders can only be placed during your business hours. No orders can be completed if the store hours are marked as closed, and WooCommerce checkout is disabled for the consumer.
